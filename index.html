About Me

Love the Outdoors!

I live for the outdoors! Everytime I hear the sound of my leash I run to the front-door and ready my paws to be put in my harness.

Picky Eater

I am a very picky eater! I refuse to eat kibble by itself and I demand that it be mixed with a meat flavour like Cesar. I'd be glad to take your unwanted chicken at the dining table!

I am Trained

I graduated PetSmart's beginner training program in July 2023. I know how to sit, shake paws, offer my chin, leave it, take it, etc. I still have trouble on staying when I see another dog in public though.
