A New Home!

Post by Hershey, August 2022.
Stouffville, Ontario

On August 22, 2022, I got to meet my forever family for the first time. When I met them, I was very excited but also sad. I was happy to meet my new family but I was sad to meet my birth mom and dad, as well as my 2 other siblings. When I got to my forever home, it was a new environment with a lot more freedom! I was afraid of the robot vaccum and was nervous around the new people I would soon call my family.
